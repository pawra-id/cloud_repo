Welcome to Pawra!

Pawra, a dedicated social startup, aims to heighten awareness of pet well-being and mental health through positive care initiatives. Our core goal is to foster positive pet wellness by bridging advocates with the broader pet community through impactful campaigns. Join us in creating a space where our shared passion for pets transforms into tangible, positive outcomes for their overall well-being. Together, let's champion a community that prioritizes the health and happiness of our beloved pets.
